The Street Lawyer is a legal thriller novel by John Grisham. It was Grisham's ninth novel. The book was released in the United States on 1 January 1998, published by Bantam Books,[1] and on 30 March 1998 in the UK, published by Century.[2]The Street Lawyer, a 1998 work of crime fiction by John Grisham, follows a lawyer named Michael Brock who investigates in the wake of a violent break-in at a law office in Washington, D.C. Taken as one of the hostages, he is curious about the motive behind the crime and launches his own private investigation. Along the way, he resolves to shift his career path, undergoing a personal transformation as he realizes how he can intervene in the complex and brilliant lives of the people who live on the margins of American urban life.
